# Support Policy Manual

This reference stands in for support-owned policy documentation.

## Relevant Facts

- Standard refunds are available within 30 days of captured payment.
- Refunds after 30 days require finance approval.
- Fraud-related refunds require risk review.
- Each organization gets one standard 14-day free trial.
- Former paid customers require sales approval before starting another trial.
