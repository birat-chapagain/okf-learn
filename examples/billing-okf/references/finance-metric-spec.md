# Finance Metric Spec

This reference stands in for a finance-owned metric specification.

## Relevant Facts

- Net revenue equals completed paid invoice value minus refunds and chargebacks.
- Draft invoices are excluded from revenue.
- Refunds are applied using refund effective date.
- Trial accounts are excluded from net revenue and churn rate.
- Churn rate uses paid customers at the start of the period as the denominator.
